Palawan


El Nido is a town in Palawan, Philippines that has crystal-clear waters and majestic beaches. You can take a tour of the nearby islands and beaches, and enjoy the stunning scenery and sunsets. It's a perfect destination for nature lovers and people who want to relax in a peaceful environment.

What to do in El nido, Palawan: Island Hopping, explore beaches, Scuba Diving and Snorkeling, Kayaking, Sunset Watching
